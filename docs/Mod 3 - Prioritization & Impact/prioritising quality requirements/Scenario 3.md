### **Scenario 3: Financial Trading Platform**

1.  **Performance:** Trade execution latency must be less than 50ms.
2.  **Scalability:** System must handle 10,000 concurrent active traders.
3.  **Security:** All financial transactions must be encrypted and fraud-resistant.
4.  **Reliability:** Zero data loss for all executed trades.
5.  **Accuracy:** All financial calculations must be precise to 8 decimal places.
6.  **Auditability:** Every trade and account activity must be traceable and auditable.
7.  **Compliance:** Adherence to all relevant financial regulations (e.g., MiFID II, FINRA).
8.  **Disaster Recovery:** Full platform recovery within 15 minutes of a major incident.
9.  **High Availability:** 99.999% uptime for core trading functions.
10. **Data Consistency:** Real-time market data must be consistent across all user interfaces.
11. **Low Latency:** Market data feeds must be processed and displayed within 100ms.
12. **User Experience:** Intuitive interface for complex trading strategies.
