### **Scenario 5: Educational Learning Platform (e.g., Online Courseware)**

1.  **Scalability:** Support 100,000 concurrent students during peak exam periods.
2.  **Performance:** Video lectures and interactive exercises must load instantly.
3.  **Accessibility:** Platform must be usable by students with disabilities (e.g., screen reader compatibility).
4.  **Reliability:** Student progress and grades must be consistently saved and available.
5.  **Security:** Protect student personal identifiable information (PII) and academic records.
6.  **Personalization:** Provide adaptive learning paths based on student performance.
7.  **Usability:** Intuitive navigation for students of all technical proficiencies.
8.  **Maintainability:** Course content updates should be deployable weekly without service interruption.
9.  **Integration:** Connect with external university student information systems (SIS).
10. **Collaboration:** Support real-time chat and forum discussions for students.
11. **Reporting:** Generate detailed analytics on student engagement and learning outcomes for instructors.
12. **Offline Access:** Allow students to download course materials for offline study.
