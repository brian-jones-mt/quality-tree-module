### **Scenario 4: Smart City Traffic Management System**

1.  **Real-time Processing:** Traffic sensor data must be processed within 100ms.
2.  **Scalability:** Must integrate data from 10,000 traffic sensors across the city.
3.  **Reliability:** System must operate continuously without failure for 99.9% of the time.
4.  **Data Accuracy:** Traffic flow predictions should have an accuracy of 95% for the next 15 minutes.
5.  **Security:** Protection against cyber-attacks that could manipulate traffic signals.
6.  **Interoperability:** Ability to share data with emergency services and public transport systems.
7.  **Maintainability:** New traffic light schedules can be deployed without system downtime.
8.  **Geospatial Integration:** Display real-time traffic data on a city map with high precision.
9.  **Fault Tolerance:** Continue basic operations even if some sensors or communication lines fail.
10. **Energy Efficiency:** Minimize power consumption of roadside units.
11. **User Interface:** Provide clear visualizations of traffic hotspots for operators.
12. **Historical Data:** Store 5 years of traffic data for analytical purposes.
